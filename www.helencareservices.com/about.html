About Us

Our Mission
Helencare is devoted to providing personal support to individuals with disabilities in all areas of their life. We are committed to helping individuals stay connected, stay integrated and safe within the community and their own home. Participant's are provided optimal support to build their interpersonal skills and build confidence to navigate through life.
Our Vision
Our vision is to provide services such as respite care to individual's regardless of their disability, race, religion, ethnic or sexual orientation. At Precious Flower Healthcare, we foster growth and independence by helping individuals to have the freedom to make choices and decisions that enable them to achieve self-determination.
Our Philosophy
Helencare is built on the values of caring, compassion and innovation. The quality of our personal support services ensure that this new organization provides effective solutions to the ever- growing demand for home and community based healthcare.

We deliver the best quality care possible, that speaks care to the clients and families that we serve, ensuring how much they are valued and cared for as individuals. We work to restore confidence, dignity and a sense of self- worth through encouragement , kindness and open communication. Precious Flower Healthcare promotes inclusion and integration through community involvement. Ultimately, our goal is to improve and enhance the quality of life of those in our care.
